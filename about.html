About C&A

Nairobi's stall for genuine sneakers, built on trust.

Our Story

C&A started in 2021 as a small stall at Gikomba Shoe Market with one simple goal: sell genuine sneakers at fair prices, without the guesswork that comes with buying shoes on the street.

What began as a single rack of imported sneakers has grown into one of the most trusted shoe stalls in the area, serving students, hustlers, and sneakerheads across Nairobi. We still source every pair ourselves, check it for quality, and sell it at a price that makes sense.

No middlemen marking up prices. No fake "original" labels. Just real shoes, sold honestly.
