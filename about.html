About C&A

Nairobi's stall for genuine sneakers, built on trust.

Our Story

C&A started in 2021 as a small stall at Sarit Centre in Westlands with one simple goal: sell genuine sneakers at fair prices, without the guesswork that comes with buying shoes on the street.

What began as a single rack of imported sneakers has grown into one of the most trusted shoe stalls in the area, serving students, hustlers and sneakerheads across Nairobi.

No middlemen marking up prices. No fake "original" labels. Just real shoes, sold honestly.
